### Agno framework
A lean, developer-friendly agent orchestration layer for building practical AI systems quickly. When compared to complex and feature rich frameworks like LangChain where both are designed for LLM-based applications supporting agents, tools and chaining
Agno is simpler,lightweight and easier to reason about.


## Key points
* Agent-first design - Everything revolves around AI agents that can reason, plan, and act.
* Tool integration - Agents can connect to APIs, databases, or functions (e.g., search, calculators, internal tools).
* Composable workflows - You can chain agents or steps together to create multi-step pipelines.
* Minimal abstraction - Keeps things simple—less “magic,” more control for developers.
* LLM-agnostic - Works with multiple model providers (not locked into one ecosystem).


### Limitations of Agno
* Smaller ecosystem (fewer plugins, community resources)
* Less mature compared to established frameworks
* May require more manual setup for advanced use cases
* Limited built-in support for production-grade scaling (vs bigger frameworks)
